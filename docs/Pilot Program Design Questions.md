# **Pilot Design Questions**

## **1\. Purpose & Outcomes**

• What problem are we testing a solution for?

• What is the intended change (short-term outcome)?

• What will success look like after the pilot?

## **2\. People**

• Who is the target participant? (eligibility criteria, demographics, number served)

• Who are the staff, volunteers, or trainers needed? (roles, qualifications)

• Who else needs to be involved? (partners, advisors, community leaders)

## **3\. Activities & Design** 

• What exactly happens in the pilot? (services, curriculum, sessions, events)

• Where will it take place? (venue, online platform, community site)

• How often? (frequency, duration, intensity)

• How many? (scale of sessions, groups, or activities)

## **4\. Resources & Supplies**

• What materials or equipment are required? (computers, food, space, curricula)

• What are the direct costs? (staff, supplies, incentives)

• What are the indirect costs? (insurance, admin support, transportation)

## **5\. Timing & Scale**

• How long will the pilot run? (weeks, months, cycles)

• What are the start and end dates?

• How many people will you serve?

• How does this relate to your larger vision?
